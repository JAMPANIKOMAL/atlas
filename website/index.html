ATLAS is an AI-driven pipeline for taxonomic identification and discovery from environmental DNA (eDNA), reducing the "database gap" in biodiversity research.

Our Core Mission

The ATLAS project addresses a critical challenge in modern biodiversity research: the "database gap." Standard reference databases are often incomplete, hindering the identification of many organisms.

Our AI-driven pipeline minimizes reliance on these databases, reduces computational time, and uniquely enables the discovery of novel taxa from raw eDNA reads.

Our AI Pipelines

The ATLAS system is built on specialized pipelines for different genetic markers.

16S Pipeline

Our robust pipeline for identifying Bacteria and Archaea, utilizing the well-established 16S rRNA marker.

Target: Prokaryotes
Test Accuracy: 88.95%
Key Feature: Robust K-mer size of 6

18S Pipeline

A specialized pipeline for identifying Eukaryotic organisms, with a unique parser to handle complex taxonomic strings.

Target: Eukaryotes
Test Accuracy: 64.09%
Key Feature: Advanced taxonomy parser

COI Pipeline

Our most accurate pipeline, focused on identifying animal species using the Cytochrome Oxidase I gene.

Target: Animalia
Test Accuracy: 96.87%
Key Feature: Robust BOLD data parsing

ITS Pipeline

A pipeline designed for the complex and variable fungal kingdom, using the ITS region for identification.

Target: Fungi
Test Accuracy: 62.19%
Key Feature: UNITE database parser

Explorer Pipeline

Our cutting-edge unsupervised learning pipeline that discovers novel taxa and unknown genetic variants in environmental samples.

Methodology: Doc2Vec & HDBSCAN
Mission: Overcoming the "database gap"
Discovery: Clustering novel taxa

How it Works

Follow our revolutionary journey from environmental samples to actionable biodiversity insights

Sample Collection

We begin by collecting environmental DNA (eDNA) from water, soil, or air samples using standardized field protocols that ensure sample integrity.

DNA Sequencing & Barcoding

In the lab, we extract the eDNA and amplify key genetic barcode regions using next-generation sequencing technology.

AI Analysis with ATLAS

Our proprietary ATLAS AI platform transforms raw sequence data into accurate classifications using deep learning models trained on genetic "fingerprints."

Data Visualization & Reports

AI-driven classifications are synthesized into interactive dashboards and comprehensive biodiversity reports for actionable insights.
